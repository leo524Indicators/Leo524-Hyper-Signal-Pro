**Smart Money Engine by Leo524**

Leo524 Liquidity Hunter PRO is a high-performance Pine Script v5 indicator built for traders who focus on liquidity, market structure, and institutional behavior. It is designed to identify high-probability zones where price is likely to react, helping traders execute smarter and safer entries.

## 🧩 How It Works

This indicator tracks liquidity zones where stop orders accumulate. When price sweeps these levels, the system waits for confirmation such as displacement and structure shift before highlighting potential entry zones.

The logic is designed to follow institutional behavior:

* Liquidity grab → Reaction → Confirmation → Entry zone
